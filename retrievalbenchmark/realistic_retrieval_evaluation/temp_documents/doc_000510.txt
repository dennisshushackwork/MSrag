Title: Long-term respiratory follow-up of H1N1 infection
Passage: The first human infections with the new influenza A virus were confirmed in April 2009 in America, but the infection had been rapidly spreading around the world and in June 2009 World Health Organization declared a pandemic . WHO had advised countries in the northern hemisphere to prepare for a second wave of pandemic spread . To date, almost all countries in the world have confirmed a second wave of H1N1 virus .
