Title: Health care workers indicate ill preparedness for Ebola Virus Disease outbreak in Ashanti Region of Ghana
Passage: We administered a face-to-face questionnaire using an adapted WHO and CDC Checklist for Ebola Preparedness and assessed overall knowledge gaps, and preparedness of the Ghanaian HCWs in selected health facilities of the Ashanti Region of Ghana from October to December 2015. RESULTS: A total 92 HCWs indicated they were not adequately trained to handle an EVD suspected case. Only 25.74% considered their facilities sufficiently equipped to handle and manage EVD patients.
