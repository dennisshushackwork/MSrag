Title: Population Serologic Immunity to Human and Avian H2N2 Viruses in the United States and Hong Kong for Pandemic Risk Assessment
Passage: H1N1 viruses as the cause of seasonal influenza from 1957 to 1968. In 1968 a third pandemic was caused by an H3N2 virus, which replaced H2N2 viruses and continues to circulate in humans to the present day. Influenza A pandemics are associated with significant mortality, morbidity, and financial burden.
