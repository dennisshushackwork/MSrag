Title: RNAi Therapeutic Platforms for Lung Diseases
Passage: The use of aerosols to deliver medication to the lungs has a long history. Administration by inhalation is a popular and non-invasive method of delivering agents into the lungs. There are several inhalation devices available for the delivery of drugs into the lungs. Metered dose inhalers and dry powder inhalers are the most common modes of inhaled delivery.
