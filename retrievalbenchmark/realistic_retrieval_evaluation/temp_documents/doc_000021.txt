Title: Clinical review: Primary influenza viral pneumonia
Passage: The real-time reverse transcriptase-polymerase chain reaction Swine Flu Panel for detection of pandemic H1N1 influenza, developed by the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention and distributed to many laboratories in US and worldwide, is a reliable and timely method of diagnosing the pandemic strain . The viral culture, while the gold standard in influenza diagnostics, takes several days before the results are known . The direct fluorescent antigen influenza test was recently reported to have a sensitivity of 93% compared with the rRT-PCR , but the test requires considerable technical expertise in addition to a fluorescent microscope.
