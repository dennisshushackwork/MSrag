Title: Mortality among patients with tuberculosis requiring intensive care: a retrospective cohort study
Passage: Across the world tuberculosis remains an important public health problem, especially in developing countries. One third of the world's population is infected with Mycobacterium tuberculosis. Brazil is ranking 15 th among the 22 high-burden countries that collectively account for 80% of TB cases globally. The incidence of TB was of 50 cases/100,000 population/yr in 2006, and recently reached approximately 100 cases/ 100,000 population in the city of Porto Alegre .
