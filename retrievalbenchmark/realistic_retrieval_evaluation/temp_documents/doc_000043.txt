Title: Health care workers indicate ill preparedness for Ebola Virus Disease outbreak in Ashanti Region of Ghana
Passage: A total of 92 HCWs surveyed indicated they were not adequately trained to handle an EVD suspected case. When asked to rate their competence in handling an EVD suspected patient, 18.81% indicated they had little confidence and competence, while 6.93% indicated they were extremely confident to handle a suspected case of EVD .
